Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how FuelUp collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

FillUp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Key Points: We prioritize your privacy and only collect data necessary for the app's functionality. Your fuel tracking data remains private and secure.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, and other vehicle details you choose to enter
  • Fuel Data: Information about your fuel purchases, including gallons, price per gallon, and odometer readings
  • User Preferences: Settings for units, notifications, and other app preferences

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, feature usage, and crash reports
  • Location Data: Only if you enable location services for fuel station data (optional)

2.3 Third-Party Information

We may receive information from third-party services you connect to FillUp, such as cloud storage providers if you enable data backup.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide fuel tracking, calculations, and reporting features
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and preferences
  • Communication: To send important updates, security alerts, and support responses
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our app features
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all your fuel tracking data is stored locally on your device. This ensures maximum privacy and security of your personal information.

4.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted before transmission and stored securely on our servers. You can disable this feature at any time in the app settings.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure server infrastructure with industry-standard protections
Your Control: You have complete control over your data. You can export, delete, or transfer your information at any time.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of FillUp, our users, or the public
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with continued privacy protection)

5.3 Public Information

Any information you choose to make public (such as in app reviews or public forums) is not covered by this privacy policy.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: View all your personal data through the app
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Disable optional features like analytics and notifications

6.2 Account Management

You can manage your account settings directly in the app, including:

  • Enabling or disabling cloud backup
  • Controlling notification preferences
  • Managing data sharing settings
  • Exporting or deleting your data
GDPR and CCPA Compliance: If you are in the European Union or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively.

7. Children's Privacy

FillUp is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (if you have enabled email communications)
  • Displaying a prominent notice in the app

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Regulatory Complaints

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe our processing of your personal information violates applicable law.

Thank you for trusting FillUp with your fuel tracking data.

Your privacy is our priority, and we're committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.
