How to Use FuelUp

Get started with FuelUp in just a few simple steps. Our intuitive interface makes fuel tracking effortless.

1

Download & Install

Download FuelUp from the Google Play Store and install it on your Android device. The app is free and lightweight.

Get it on Google Play
2

Add Your Vehicle

Create a profile for your vehicle with basic information like make, model, year, and current odometer reading.

  • Vehicle details and specifications
  • Initial odometer reading
  • Fuel tank capacity
  • Preferred units (MPG/L100km)
3

Record Fill-ups

Every time you fill up, simply enter the gallons, price per gallon, and current odometer reading. FuelUp does the rest!

  • Quick fill-up entry
  • Automatic MPG calculation
  • Cost tracking and analysis
  • Receipt photo support
4

Monitor & Analyze

View detailed reports, track efficiency trends, and get insights to help you drive more efficiently and save money.

  • Real-time efficiency monitoring
  • Historical data analysis
  • Cost per mile calculations
  • Efficiency improvement tips

Pro Tips for Better Tracking

Always fill up at the same gas station for more consistent pricing data.

Record fill-ups immediately after filling up for the most accurate data.

Enable cloud backup to sync your data across multiple devices.

Use the maintenance reminders to stay on top of vehicle service.
